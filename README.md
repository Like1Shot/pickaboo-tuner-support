  ## About Pickaboo Tuner

  Pickaboo Tuner is a fast, accurate chromatic tuner for iOS. Perfect for guitars, bass, ukulele, violin, and any
  other instrument.

  ### Features
  - Real-time pitch detection
  - Smooth visual needle display
  - Waterfall visualization
  - Support for all notes (chromatic)
  - Optimized for string instruments
  - Clean, dark interface

  ---

  ## Frequently Asked Questions

  ### How do I use the tuner?
  1. Open Pickaboo Tuner
  2. Play a note on your instrument
  3. Watch the needle - center means in tune!
  4. Adjust your tuning until the needle stays green

  ### Why isn't the app detecting my instrument?
  - Make sure microphone permission is granted
  - Play closer to your device's microphone
  - Ensure you're in a quiet environment
  - For electric guitars, try playing unplugged or near an amp speaker

  ### What instruments does it support?
  Pickaboo Tuner works with any instrument! It's a chromatic tuner that detects all notes (A, A#, B, C, C#, D, D#, E,
   F, F#, G, G#).

  ### Does the app work offline?
  Yes! Pickaboo Tuner works completely offline. No internet required for tuning.

  ## Privacy Policy

  Pickaboo Tuner respects your privacy.

  **Data We Collect:**
  - Microphone audio (processed locally, never stored or transmitted)

  **Third-Party Services:**
  - Google AdMob (displays ads, may collect device identifiers for ad personalization)

  We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. Audio from your microphone is processed in real-time
  on your device and is never recorded or sent anywhere.

  For AdMob's privacy practices, see: [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
