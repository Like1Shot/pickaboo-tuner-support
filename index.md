  # Pickaboo Tuner Support

  Welcome to the official support page for **Pickaboo Tuner** - your chromatic tuning companion.

  ## About the App

  Pickaboo Tuner is a precise chromatic tuner for musicians. Tune any instrument with real-time pitch detection and a
   beautiful, easy-to-read interface.

  ## Features

  - Chromatic tuning for all instruments
  - Real-time pitch detection
  - Visual tuning needle and waterfall display
  - Note detection with cent accuracy
  - Clean, modern interface

  ## Support

  ### Frequently Asked Questions

  **Q: Why does the app need microphone access?**
  A: The microphone is used to detect the pitch of your instrument. Audio is processed entirely on your device and is
   never recorded or transmitted.

  **Q: What instruments can I tune?**
  A: Pickaboo is a chromatic tuner, meaning it can detect any musical note. It works with guitar, bass, ukulele,
  violin, viola, cello, wind instruments, and more.

  **Q: Is an internet connection required?**
  A: No, the tuner works completely offline. Internet is only used to display ads.

  **Q: How accurate is the tuner?**
  A: Pickaboo Tuner detects pitch with accuracy within ±1 cent.
